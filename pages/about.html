About Jaggu Kashyap

Jaggu Kashyap is the founder and author behind ARTICH, a storytelling brand focused on real-life moments, resilience, and everyday inspiration. His writing style blends emotion, authenticity, and practical wisdom to connect with readers from all backgrounds.

Through ARTICH, Jaggu explores themes of growth, relationships, memories, and modern life, aiming to make each book feel personal and memorable.
