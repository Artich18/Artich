Jaggu Kashyap

Author | Creator | Storyteller

Jaggu Kashyap is the founder and author behind ARTICH, a storytelling brand focused on real-life moments, resilience, and everyday inspiration. His writing blends emotion, authenticity, and real experiences.

My name is Jaggu Kashyap. I am a writer and storyteller. I do not write to create imaginary stories. I write about things that I have seen, felt, and experienced in real life.

My journey of writing did not start with books or fame. It started during a very difficult time in my life. There were days when life was so hard that I had to spend nights on the streets, on open grounds, and sometimes under the open sky.

At that time, I did not know that those struggles and experiences would one day become the source of my writing.

I believe that every person has many stories inside them. But most people never get a chance to share those stories. Pain, silence, struggles, and hopes often stay hidden inside our hearts.

Through my writing, I try to give words to those feelings that people often cannot express.

My book “Maa, Main Theek Hoon” is also inspired by real emotions. It is not just a story. It reflects the feelings of many people who hide their pain and simply say to their loved ones:

“Mom, I’m fine.”

Through my stories, I hope readers feel that someone understands what they are going through.

ARTICH is not just a name for me. It is a place where real emotions, life experiences, and human stories are shared through words.

If my story or my book makes someone stop for a moment and feel something real, then my writing has done its job.
